About

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science) at UEM Kolkata provides cutting-edge education and research in the field of Data Science. Our mission is to foster innovation, encourage analytical thinking, and empower students to become leaders in industry and academia. We offer top-notch infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry collaborations to ensure an enriching learning environment.
